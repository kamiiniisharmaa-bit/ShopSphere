Connecting the world through meaningful commerce

More than a marketplace — a global community of creators, dreamers, and conscious shoppers.

ShopSphere was born from the idea that shopping should inspire, empower, and connect. We bring together unique products from independent sellers and global brands, all in one seamless sphere.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 in New York City, ShopSphere started as a small collective of artisans and tech enthusiasts who believed in a better way to shop. What began as a curated marketplace for sustainable goods has grown into a trusted destination for millions.

Our mission is simple: empower independent sellers, delight customers with quality, and build a platform that puts people and planet first. Today, ShopSphere connects over 10,000 sellers to 2 million+ customers across 45+ countries.

We're proud to be a certified B Corp candidate, committed to ethical sourcing, carbon-neutral shipping, and giving back 1% of sales to global education initiatives. Every purchase on ShopSphere makes a difference.
