1. Information We Collect

ShopSphere collects information to provide, improve, and personalize your shopping experience. The categories of data we collect include:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Identity DataName, email address, phone number, shipping addressAccount creation, order fulfillment, customer support
Payment DataCard details, billing address (processed via PCI-compliant partners)Process transactions, prevent fraud
Transaction DataPurchase history, wishlist, returns, reviewsOrder management, personalized recommendations
Technical DataIP address, browser type, device IDs, cookiesSite security, analytics, performance optimization
Profile DataPreferences, feedback, survey responsesMarketing (with consent), improving services

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., health, biometric, political beliefs) unless you voluntarily provide it.

2. How We Use Your Information

ShopSphere uses your data for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order processing & delivery: Confirm purchases, ship items, and handle returns.
  • Customer support: Respond to inquiries, resolve disputes, and provide assistance.
  • Personalization: Recommend products based on browsing and purchase history.
  • Marketing communications: Send promotions, offers, and newsletters (you may opt out anytime).
  • Legal compliance: Fraud detection, tax reporting, and responding to lawful requests.
  • Platform improvement: Analyze trends, test features, and enhance security.

3. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

ShopSphere does not sell your personal data. We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Shipping carriers (USPS, FedEx), payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), cloud hosting, and analytics (Google Analytics).
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of ShopSphere or its users.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your data may be transferred with notice.
  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing.

No third-party marketing: We never sell your email or personal data to advertisers.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

ShopSphere uses cookies, local storage, and web beacons to enhance functionality and analyze traffic. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for cart, checkout, and account security.
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics, Hotjar — help us understand user behavior.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember language and region settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for retargeting campaigns (you can opt out).
We value your privacy. You can manage cookie preferences.

You can also disable cookies via your browser settings, but some site features may not function properly.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., California, EU, UK, Brazil), you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of direct marketing and certain profiling.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@shopsphere.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

ShopSphere implements industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, firewalls, regular vulnerability scans, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication where available. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

ShopSphere does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately. We will delete such information promptly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records for 7 years), resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. After retention periods expire, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. International Data Transfers

ShopSphere operates globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date indicates the latest revision. Material changes will be notified via email, website banner, or pop-up. Your continued use of ShopSphere after changes signifies acceptance. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
